Some time ago I remember trying to peek up nature's skirt,
Curious and stupid as I was and should've been,
And I found my mother, my aunts, my ancestors and myself
All deriving from this strange and curious portal to life,
And all of the poetry started to leave me...

So I thought less of where I came from and decided to peek
