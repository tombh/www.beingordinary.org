In this special hour long podcast Mike travels to Tirruvanamalai in India to meet Sri V. Ganesan, a grand-nephew of Ramana Maharshi and spiritual teacher in his own right. Ganesan's own journey included meeting sages such as J. Krishnamurti, Nisargadatta Maharaj, Yogi Ramsuratkumar and Robert Adams who helped him reveal the essence of Ramana's message. We hope you enjoy this one, questions and comments are welcome below.
