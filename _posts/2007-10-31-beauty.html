Looking out of my window I am blessed by the sight of a tree, shining yellow in its Autumnal beauty. I am reminded of Ikkyu who wrote "Oh green green willow, wonderfully red flower, but I know the colours are not there."

Yet not only are the colours not there but there is no one to see them.

Yet seeing happens and that, is beauty.
