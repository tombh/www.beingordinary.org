Hidden behind the top of a street
was a view that made me smile.
Concealed within the maths of matter
was a bomb that changed the world.

 

It is not just our eyes that see,
but our hands and hearts and minds.

 

Hidden behind a habit
was a feeling that made me grow.
Concealed within an ordinary life
is the spark that changes the universe.

 

Thus ignited, the ensuing detonations
obliterate more obstacles than any nuclear weapon.
