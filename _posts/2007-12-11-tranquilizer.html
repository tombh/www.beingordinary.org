The panic isn’t as sustainable as it used to be
The heart just purrs rather than pounds
Basics physics probably explains the phenomenon simply
When you’re traveling at the speed of light all things slow down
Becoming relative 

I must have slowed down
Missed the bus
Couldn’t moveFor a lack of basic reasoning 

Stuck – glued to a spot where acceleration
Forgets to occur
The panic just leaves me
When I trap all of the splattered patterns of the sky
Tomorrow is definitely an option 

Make a vow to complete this
This acrimony
Break it into tiny bits to reassemble at a
Point where…
The…
Futures don’t...
Disturb the dust so...
Quickly.
