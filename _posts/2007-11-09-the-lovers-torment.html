Do they who really love, suffer from loving?

When they love the world so much as to want to join both ends together?

When they love the world so much they wouldn't dare bothering it

Talking about Love itself?

If you know the answer, come cry with Benjamin.

Because these tears will make the moon feel shy tonight.

Until the sun rises again.
