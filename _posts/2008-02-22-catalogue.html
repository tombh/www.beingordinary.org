Boxes of jewells and rocks weeding gowns no typo
No clarity no dignity no poetry just rhymes
just words

No punctuation no! We don't need
Sentence structure here/hear
We have grown bored of
Such things as words
We catalogue more than the Dewey decimal system allows.

I read my DNA the other day
And to my great relief it told me
Exactly who I was, am and will be
For the rest of my life.

I'd better get on with it then and forget about this nonsense;
Someone else has already figured out who I am.

Thanks for that - I thought it may require some effort on my part.
How wrong was I?
