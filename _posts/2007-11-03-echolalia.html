Chaos and order.
Left and right.
It’s already starting to sound moral.
There’s a message there,
somewhere;
a tangible truth.
A meaning.
A reason.

North and south.
Odds and evens.
Opposites attract.
Smoking kills.
There’s a message.
Proof.
An abstract goal.
Some discernible truth,
maybe.
Perhaps.
Occasionally.
Search and find.
Repeat and fade.
