Headlong into the furnace I toss my soul
Into dusty corners picking pieces of memory out
At breakneck speeds I swallow my gathered thoughts
Crumpled together in strange document formats
DO YOU WANT TO SAVE?

The keys confuse me - <ENTER>
I see little that is unlocked
So I attempt to <RETURN>
I doesn't work
So I try to Escape... Shift... Space... Control... Alter...
