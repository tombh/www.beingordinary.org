Amnesty could be a plague,
For we lack truth and agreement -
What is true?

Has the world suddenly become so large,
Has the media and the medium become
So disagreeable that we must come to some
Disproportionate agreement about what
Constitutes a person?

How to govern six billion... nine billion
And so much agreed capital as to how to govern...
Has the world really become that unmanagable?
Has the forth Estate become the first Estate?
Is the management enough?

Rue the day that I cannot love for lack of bread,
Kill the man that killed the baker,
The good is only as good as you agree that it is -
All else is vanity.
