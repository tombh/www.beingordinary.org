Choices.
What’s wrong with my choices?
THEY’RE MY FUCKING CHOICES.
If you don’t like them, DON’T LIKE THEM!
It doesn’t change anything.
If you don’t like the choices you’ve made…
TOO BAD, you’ve already made them.
Regret them or don’t.
Whatever you think you have time for.
Whatever you think will help.
Whatever you believe will serve you in some way.

Recognise the similarities between what you’re going through,
And what others are going through in order to feel acceptable.
Recognise the differences between what you’re going through,
And what others are going through in order to feel individual.

Make up the rest.
Design a new complex.
Read a different opinion.
Read a similar opinion.
Think a lot, or not.
Choose to change your choices.

Choices. What’s wrong with my CHOICES?

Nothing. Everything.

Something.

Whatever.
