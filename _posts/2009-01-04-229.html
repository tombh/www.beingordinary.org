Reflections of nothing

Must look like something

If the law of opposites is to be believed.

In which case I am always nowhere,

Yet, craftily,

I clothe myself in mountains and stars,

As a desolate drunk, stubborn in self-loathing,

As a smiling child, waiting for the next hee-hee-hee.
